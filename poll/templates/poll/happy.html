How Tallymeet Works

Tallymeet compares everyone's schedule and ranks time slots based on availability.

1: Create a poll

List the time slots/range for the group's consideration.

2: Share the poll

Group members respond to time slots according to their schedule.

3: Results

Tallymeet ranks the time slots and displays the results.

Features

No Registration Required

You do not need an account to create, share, or access Tallymeet polls. However, an account allows you to save your polls, so you can have everything in one place.

Share Your Link

After creating a poll, simply share the link with the group for voting. Remember, no registration required.

Ranked Time Slots

Participants indicate their availability by responding to a time slot in three ways: "prefer", "yes", or "no". You can then sort the responses to determine a suitable time slot for your group.
