How Tallymeet Works

Tallymeet compares everyone's schedule and ranks time slots based on availability.

1: Create a Tallymeet poll

List the time slots/range for the group's consideration.

2: Share the poll

Group members pick time slots according to their schedule.

3: Results

Tallymeet ranks the time slots and displays the results on the poll.

No Registration Required

You do not need an account to create, share, or access Tallymeet polls. However, an account allows you to save your polls, so you can have everything in one place.

Free of Charge

Tallymeet is a free service. Create as many polls as you want.

Predefined and Heatmap Polls

With predefined polls, the host specifies the time slots for the group to choose from. With heatmap polls (coming soon), the group collectively comes up with suitable time slots over a range of time.
